Installation Guide

Step-by-step instructions to set up your EzHome smart devices

Before You Begin

Note: Ensure you have a stable Wi-Fi connection and the EzHome mobile app installed before starting the installation process.
  • Download the EzHome app from Google Play Store
  • Create your EzHome account
  • Ensure your Wi-Fi network is 2.4GHz compatible
  • Have your Wi-Fi password ready

Installation Steps by Device

  1. Unpack the Echo Hub

    Remove the Echo Hub from its packaging and locate the power adapter.

  2. Connect to Power

    Plug the power adapter into the Echo Hub and connect to a wall outlet. The LED will turn blue when powering on.

  3. Open EzHome App

    Launch the EzHome app and tap "Add Device" then select "Echo Hub".

  4. Follow Setup Wizard

    The app will guide you through Wi-Fi connection and voice training.

  5. Test Voice Commands

    Try saying "Hey EzHome, what's the weather?" to confirm setup is complete.

Tips
  • Place hub centrally in your home
  • Keep away from other electronics
  • Ensure clear line of sight
  • Setup takes about 5-10 minutes

  1. Install Bulbs

    Screw the smart LED bulbs into your existing light fixtures. Ensure power is off before installation.

  2. Turn On Power

    Switch on the light fixture. The bulb will flash briefly to indicate it's ready for pairing.

  3. Add to App

    In the EzHome app, tap "Add Device" → "Lighting" → "Smart LED Bulb".

  4. Scan for Devices

    The app will automatically detect nearby bulbs. Select your bulb from the list.

  5. Name Your Bulb

    Give your bulb a memorable name like "Living Room Lamp" or "Bedroom Light".

Common Issues & Solutions

Wi-Fi Connection Issues
  • Ensure your network is 2.4GHz
  • Check Wi-Fi password is correct
  • Move device closer to router
  • Restart your router
App Not Detecting Device
  • Enable Bluetooth on your phone
  • Grant location permissions to app
  • Reset device to factory settings
  • Update the EzHome app
Voice Commands Not Working
  • Check microphone isn't muted
  • Retrain voice recognition
  • Reduce background noise
  • Update device firmware
Device Won't Turn On
  • Check power connections
  • Verify outlet is working
  • Try different power adapter
  • Contact customer support
