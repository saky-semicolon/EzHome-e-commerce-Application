How to Use the App

Step-by-step guide to navigate EzHome

Search Products

Use the search bar on the homepage to find specific smart home products. Type keywords like "smart bulb", "security camera", or browse by categories.

Tip: Use specific product names for better results
Shopping Cart

Click "Add to Cart" on any product page. View your cart by clicking the cart icon in the navigation bar. Manage quantities and remove items as needed.

Tip: Cart items are saved even if you leave the page
Checkout

When ready to purchase, click "Checkout" in your cart. Fill in your shipping information, select payment method, and confirm your order.

Tip: Create an account to save payment info
Login & Register

Create an account or log in to save your information, track orders, and access exclusive features. Click Login/Register in the top navigation.

Tip: Registered users get faster checkout
