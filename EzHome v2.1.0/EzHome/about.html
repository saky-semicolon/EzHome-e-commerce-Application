Founded by Visionary

EzHome was founded in 2022 by Gabor Berth, a Computer Science graduate from Murdoch University (Kaplan).

EzHome was launched with a goal to become Singapore's one stop marketplace for all smart home devices. By partnering with trusted suppliers, EzHome ensures that customers have access to a wide range of products and to become the preferred choice for homeowners.
