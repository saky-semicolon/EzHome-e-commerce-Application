Easy returns and hassle-free refunds for your EzHome purchases

How to Return Your Product

1
Initiate Return

Log into your account and click "Return Item" or contact customer service.

2
Print Label

We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label within 24 hours.

3
Package & Ship

Pack the item in original packaging and drop off at any shipping location.

4
Get Refund

Receive your refund within 3-5 business days after we receive the item.

Return Conditions

Eligible for Return
  • Items in original packaging
  • All accessories included
  • Product in new/like-new condition
  • Returned within 30 days
  • Original receipt or order number
  • Unopened software/firmware
Not Eligible for Return
  • Items damaged by misuse
  • Products with missing parts
  • Items returned after 30 days
  • Personalized/customized products
  • Items damaged by pets
  • Products with permanent installation

Refund Information

Refund Methods
  • Original payment method
  • Bank transfer (international)
  • Store credit option available
Processing Times
  • Credit Card: 3-5 business days
  • PayPal: 1-3 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 5-7 business days
Note: Refunds are processed after we receive and inspect the returned item. You'll receive an email confirmation once your refund has been processed.
